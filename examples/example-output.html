🗣 一场乌龙面试，反而让我更坚定了

2026年7月3日 · 工作 · 面试 · 梦想 · 约 290 词

📝 口语稿 Script

So, today was… honestly, a pretty wild day. I took the afternoon off work to go to a job interview — it was supposed to be at this German company. I was actually really excited because, like, I've always dreamed of working for a foreign company. I genuinely believe they respect their employees more and actually follow labor laws, you know? So I showed up feeling prepared and confident, and… get this — the address was completely fake. I got there and it was literally just a storage room. A storage room! I was like, you've gotta be kidding me. I just stood there feeling this mix of disappointment and absurdity（荒谬）. Part of me wanted to laugh, part of me was like, what just happened?

But here's the thing — weirdly enough, this whole experience actually gave me some clarity. It made me realize that I genuinely have the drive to go after what I want. I didn't just sit around thinking about it — I actually went out there and took a shot. So instead of feeling defeated, I honestly feel more determined than ever. My plan now is to nail the IELTS this year, keep sharpening my skills, and the next time a real foreign company has an opening in my field, I'm jumping on it. No hesitation.

As for my current job… it's been pretty draining, honestly. A lot of the senior people there love to pull rank — you know, throw their seniority （资历） around and shoot down every idea I bring up without even thinking about it. It's like if you haven't been there for years, your opinion doesn't count. But honestly? That's just fuel. Every time they dismiss me, I wanna prove myself even more. This fake interview didn't break my spirit — if anything, it showed me that my dream is real, and I'm ready to chase it.

💡 常用表达 Key Expressions
📖 对话背景：满怀期待去一家"德企"面试，结果地址是假的、现场是个杂物间。从荒谬和失望中，反而看清了自己真正想要什么。
  • get this 地道表达

    意思是"你听听这个"、"你猜怎么着"，用来引出让人惊讶或难以置信的事情。比 "guess what" 更有戏剧感。

    美国人讲故事时高频使用，像是给听众一个"坐稳了"的预警。

  • I was like, "..." 句型骨架

    意思是"我当时就觉得..."、"我心想..."，引出口语中的直接反应。比 "I thought" 更自然、更有画面感。

    日常对话里几乎每三句话就会出现一次，用熟了你的口语瞬间变地道。

  • you've gotta be kidding me 地道表达

    "你在跟我开玩笑吧！"表达难以置信、荒唐到想笑的感觉。可以单独成句，非常有冲击力。

    语气介于愤怒和苦笑之间——正好适合"哭笑不得"的场景。

  • take a shot (at something) 地道表达

    意思是"试一把"、"赌一下"，比 "try" 更有冲劲和勇气。隐含"不一定成功但我愿意冒险"的意思。

    比如：I wasn't sure I'd get the job, but I figured I'd take a shot.

  • pull rank 地道表达

    意思是"摆架子"、"仗着资历压人"。精确描述那种利用职位/年资来压制别人的行为。

    源自军队用语（rank = 军衔），现在职场里非常常用。

  • shoot down (someone's ideas) 地道表达

    意思是"直接毙掉/否决（别人的想法）"，比 "reject" 更有画面感——像开枪打下来一样干脆。

    注意这里的 shoot down 是比喻用法，不是字面意思。

  • jump on it 地道表达

    意思是"立刻抓住（机会）"、"毫不犹豫地行动"。充满果断和行动力。

    比如：If a foreign company offers me a position, I'm jumping on it. 强调毫不犹豫。

  • That's just fuel. 句型骨架

    字面意思是"那只是燃料"，实际意思是"那只会成为我的动力"。用 fuel（燃料）来比喻让你更有干劲的东西，非常地道。

    可以扩展：That's just fuel for me to work harder / prove myself / keep going.
